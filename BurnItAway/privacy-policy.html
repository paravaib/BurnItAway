Privacy Policy - BurnItAway

Last Updated: January 2025

Your Privacy Matters: BurnItAway is designed with privacy as a core principle. We believe your mental health journey should be completely private and secure.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 No Personal Information Required

BurnItAway does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information. You can use the app completely anonymously.

1.2 Local Data Only

The following data is stored locally on your device:

  • Worry Text: Text you enter for symbolic release (stored locally only)
  • Photos: Photos you select for symbolic burning (stored locally only)
  • App Preferences: Settings like sound, haptics, reminders
  • Usage Data: Daily ritual count, subscription status

2. How We Use Your Information

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • We cannot access your personal thoughts, worries, or photos
  • We cannot track your usage patterns
  • We cannot share your data with third parties
  • Your data never leaves your device

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

All your data is stored securely on your device using iOS's built-in security features:

  • Data is encrypted using iOS Keychain services
  • No data is transmitted to our servers
  • No cloud backup of your personal data

3.2 Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by:

  • Uninstalling the app (removes all local data)
  • Using the app's reset function in settings
  • Clearing app data in iOS Settings

4. Subscription and Payment Information

4.1 Apple App Store

Subscription payments are processed through Apple's App Store:

  • We do not store your payment information
  • Apple handles all payment processing
  • We only receive confirmation of subscription status

4.2 Subscription Data

We only store:

  • Whether you have an active subscription
  • Subscription expiration date
  • No personal identification information

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 No Third-Party Analytics

BurnItAway does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

5.2 No Social Media Integration

We do not integrate with social media platforms or share any data with them.

6. Children's Privacy

BurnItAway is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Data Breach Notification

Since we don't store your personal data on our servers, there is no risk of data breaches affecting your personal information.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (it's stored on your device)
  • Delete your data (uninstall the app)
  • Modify your data (edit within the app)
  • Export your data (copy text/photos from the app)

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

10. Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with:

  • Apple's App Store Review Guidelines
  • iOS Privacy Requirements
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) principles
